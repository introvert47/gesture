Interactive 3D Hand-Tracked Particle System is a cutting-edge, web-based immersive experience that merges GPU-accelerated 3D graphics with machine learning. By utilizing your device's webcam, the application captures your physical hand gestures in real time and translates them into fluid manipulations of over 15,000 glowing particles.

Built entirely with Three.js and MediaPipe Hands, it bridges the physical and digital worlds entirely within the browser—requiring no installations, plugins, or external hardware. A sleek, neon-purple digital skeleton mirrors your hand structure in real-time, giving you the sensation of directly bending a cosmic dust cloud to your will. It is fully optimized, highly stable, and designed to run smoothly on both desktop computers and mobile devices.

How to Use the Website
Using the website is entirely touchless and intuitive. Follow these simple steps to start interacting:

1. Setup and Permissions
Allow Camera Access: When you open the website, your browser will prompt you for permission to use your webcam. Grant access to begin. (Note: Your video feed is processed entirely locally on your device for absolute privacy; no data is sent to a server).

Position Yourself: Stand or sit so that your hand is clearly visible within the camera's frame. You will see a small, mirrored webcam preview box in the bottom right corner of your screen to help you align your position.

Lighting: For the best experience, ensure your room is decently lit so the camera can accurately trace your fingers.

2. Master the Gestures
Once the neon purple lines attach to your hand on screen, you can control the entire particle system using four simple behaviors:

👋 Move Your Hand (Attraction Mode): Move your hand anywhere across the screen. The particles are magnetized to your hand and will elegantly follow your movements through 3D space.

👌 Pinch & Open (Scale Control): Bring your index finger and thumb together in a pinch gesture to compress the particles tightly into your palm. Open your hand wide to repel the particles and watch them expand dramatically.

↔️ Wave Left to Right (Color Shifting): Move your hand horizontally from side to side. As your hand glides across the screen, the baseline hue cycles through a vibrant, glowing HSL color spectrum.

🖐️ Finger Counting (Shape Morphing): Hold up a specific number of fingers toward the camera to instantly command the particle cloud to morph into a different 3D structure:

1 Finger: Transforms the particles into a beautiful, flowing 3D Heart.

2 Fingers: Rearranges the system into a 5-Petal Flower pattern.

3 Fingers: Creates Saturn, featuring a dense planetary core surrounded by a cosmic ring.

4 Fingers: Triggers an explosive Firework trail expanding outward in all directions.

5 Fingers (Open Hand): Returns the particles to a uniform, floating Spherical Cloud matrix.
